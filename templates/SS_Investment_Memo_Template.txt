SS INVESTMENT MEMO — TEMPLATE REFERENCE
========================================

This file documents the target structure for the generated .docx memo.
The memo_writer.py module uses this structure programmatically.


SECTION 1: INVESTMENT SUMMARY
------------------------------
- Property name, address, city/state
- Key metrics table: asking price, NRSF, units, occupancy, price/SF, year built
- Screening gates table (7 gates: PASS / FAIL / TBD)
- Returns snapshot table (Bear / Base / Bull: YoC, IRR, MOIC)
- Maximum offer price headline

SECTION 2: MARKET OVERVIEW
---------------------------
- Demographics: 1/3/5-mile population, median HHI
- MSA classification (top-50 check)
- Supply assessment (new construction pipeline)
- Demand drivers (positives and negatives)
- Overall market rating: Strong / Moderate / Weak

SECTION 3: PROPERTY DESCRIPTION
---------------------------------
- Physical attributes: year built, acreage, NRSF, units, CC%
- Age narrative and condition assessment
- Replacement cost build-up table:
    Non-CC hard cost
    CC hard cost
    Site work
    Soft costs
    Developer profit
    Total replacement cost
- Price vs. replacement cost comparison

SECTION 4: FINANCIAL ANALYSIS
-------------------------------
- Income summary: GPR, vacancy, EGR, other income, total revenue
- Expense benchmarking table:
    Each line: category | CIM value | $/NRSF | benchmark range | flag
    Categories: property tax, insurance, utilities, R&M, advertising,
                payroll, G&A, management fee, capital reserve
- Analyst adjustments list (where CIM < benchmark)
- Adjusted TTM NOI with narrative
- Expense ratio check: OpEx/Revenue, OpEx/NRSF

SECTION 5: UNIT MIX & RENT ANALYSIS
-------------------------------------
- Unit mix table: size | count | SF | rate/mo | $/SF/mo | CC?
- Weighted average rent per SF
- Rent gap to market comps (if data available)
- Revenue concentration analysis

SECTION 6: VALUATION & RETURNS
--------------------------------
- For each scenario (Bear / Base / Bull):
    5-year NOI projection table
    Entry cap, exit cap, exit value
    5-year IRR, MOIC, yield on cost
    Cash flow summary
- Maximum offer price derivation

SECTION 7: VALUE-ADD OPPORTUNITIES
------------------------------------
- Ranked opportunity table: # | opportunity | est. annual impact | timeline
- Categories: revenue management (ECRI), occupancy, expense reduction,
              ancillary revenue, physical improvements
- Total estimated NOI uplift

SECTION 8: RISK ANALYSIS
--------------------------
- "Why This Deal Could Fail" — top 3-5 high-severity risks
- Full risk register table: risk | category | severity | mitigation
- Categories: market, financial, operational, screening gate, data gap, valuation
- Overall risk rating

SECTION 9: DUE DILIGENCE ITEMS
---------------------------------
Checklist:
- Actual T-12 P&L
- Population / demographic verification
- Supply pipeline confirmation
- Physical inspection
- Rent roll with move-in dates
- Property tax reassessment analysis
- Independent competitor rent survey
- Insurance quotes
- Lease agreement review
- Environmental Phase I
- Title and survey
- Zoning verification

SECTION 10: RECOMMENDATION
----------------------------
- PURSUE / DECLINE / PURSUE CONTINGENT ON
- Rationale: which gates passed/failed/TBD
- Pricing guidance: max offer vs. asking
- Key contingencies
- Disclaimer: CIM-based analysis, verify in due diligence
